Letter Descriptions

A

A closed fist, all fingers folded against the palm, thumb is straight, alongside the index finger.

B

All fingers are straight and together. Thumb is folded across palm.

C

Hand forms a curved shape like the letter C, with fingers curved and thumb opposing.

D

Index finger points up, middle finger touches thumb, other fingers folded down.

E

All fingers are bent and touching the thumb, forming a tight fist.

F

Index finger and thumb touch in a circle, other three fingers are straight up.

G

Index finger and thumb point horizontally, other fingers folded down.

H

Index and middle fingers point horizontally, other fingers folded down.

I

Pinky finger points up, other fingers folded with thumb across them.

J

Same as 'I' but with a hooking motion downward.

K

Index and middle fingers form a V, thumb touches middle finger.

L

Index finger up, thumb out horizontally, forming an L shape.

M

Three fingers draped over the thumb, pinky folded down.

N

Two fingers draped over the thumb, other fingers folded down.

O

All fingers touch the thumb, forming a circle or oval shape.

P

Similar to K, but pointing downward.

Q

Index finger and thumb point down, other fingers folded.

R

Index and middle fingers crossed, other fingers folded.

S

Fist with thumb across the fingers, similar to A but thumb in front.

T

Thumb between index and middle finger, forming a fist.

U

Index and middle fingers together, pointing up.

V

Index and middle fingers apart, forming a V shape.

W

Index, middle, and ring fingers up and apart.

X

Index finger bent in a hook shape, other fingers folded.

Y

Thumb and pinky extended, other fingers folded.

Z

Index finger traces the letter Z in the air.
